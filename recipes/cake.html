Banana Cake

Description

Put your excess bananas to good use with this homemade banana cake recipe, topped with a decadent cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • Bananas
  • Lemon juice
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • White sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Buttermilk

For the frosting:

  • Butter
  • Cream cheese
  • Vanilla extract
  • Confectioner's Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix the mashed bananas and lemon juice.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl and beat the sugar and butter in another.
  3. Beat in the eggs and the vanilla, then combine the wet and dry ingredients and stir in the banana mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a prepared pan and bake in the preheat oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. After baking, remove cake from pan and place directly into the freezer for 45 minutes.
  6. Remove from freeze and frost the cake with the frosting.
